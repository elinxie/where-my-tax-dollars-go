# Sources and interpretation notes

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21. This is a source inventory for the first release, not a substitute for the citations displayed in the product.

## Tax rules

- Internal Revenue Service, 2025 individual income-tax inflation adjustments and tax tables: [IRS Revenue Procedure 2024-40](https://www.irs.gov/irb/2024-45_IRB). Used for federal brackets, standard deductions, and payroll thresholds.
- California Franchise Tax Board, [2025 Form 540 booklet](https://www.ftb.ca.gov/forms/2025/2025-540-booklet.html). Used for California brackets, standard deductions, credits, and the mental-health surtax threshold.
- California Franchise Tax Board, [residency guidance](https://www.ftb.ca.gov/file/personal/residency-status/part-year-and-nonresident.html). Used for resident-income context.
- New Jersey Division of Taxation, [employer payroll guidance](https://www.nj.gov/treasury/taxation/businesses/payroll/index.shtml). Used only for the remote-employer warning; it does not calculate New Jersey tax.

## Spending and service context

- U.S. Department of the Treasury, [USAspending Explorer](https://www.usaspending.gov/explorer). Federal spending-category context.
- California Department of Finance, [2025–26 budget detail](https://ebudget.ca.gov/budget/e/2025-26/BudgetDetail). California budget-category context.
- County of Santa Clara, [FY 2025–26 adopted budget](https://data.sccgov.org/stories/s/Fiscal-Year-2025-26-Adopted-Budget/bqmh-662y/). County budget-category context.

## Interpretation and limits

- Budget shares are illustrative allocations of published outlays, not an accounting trace from one taxpayer to one program.
- The county chart is intentionally a budget mix only. Property tax is shared by schools, cities, the county, and special districts, so the app does not claim a personal county-dollar amount.
- The calculator supports basic W-2 scenarios. Self-employment, itemizing, credits beyond qualifying children, capital gains, and city-specific taxes require later models or a tax professional.
- Source data and formulas are versioned; corrections create a new release rather than silently replacing a prior release.
